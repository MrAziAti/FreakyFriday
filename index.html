Hi my name is Tyrons Spencer and i am a watch collector and a hobbyist photographer. I like to take pictures of everything that is about watches. I basically like every authentic watch brand but i have a favourite.

Rolex

Rolex have been my favourite for a long time now. I remember the first time i saw a Rolex, i was still in highscool. I passed by the local watch dealer and spotted the mighty Submariner in the window. The shiny steel bracelet and the black bezel cought my eye. I decided that one day that watch would be mine. You can call it a love at first sight kind of a love story. Rest is history
